About H-Thai-ML

The essence of Thai Food is all about balance - achieving the perfect harmony between sweet, sour, hot and spicy. Pungent fresh herbs, such as lemongrass and galangal, tone down overpowering spices, while salty sauces are tempred with sugars and offset by acids, such as lemon and lime.
